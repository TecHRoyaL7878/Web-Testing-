Your Privacy, Always First
All your financial data — transactions, savings, debts, and trips — is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server without your explicit action. Google Sheets sync is entirely optional and you control it completely.
📱
Works Everywhere
Built as a pure web application using modern HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no installation required. Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile browsers, with a responsive design that adapts to any screen size.
🎨
Ocean Blue Design System
Inspired by the depth and clarity of the ocean, our colour palette flows from deep royal navy (#03045E) through ocean blue (#0077B6) to the bright clarity of sky cyan (#00B4D8) — symbolising trust, intelligence, and precision.
📊
Powered by Chart.js
Interactive analytics powered by Chart.js gives you beautiful bar charts, line graphs, doughnut charts, and polar area charts — all updating in real-time as you add transactions, giving you instant financial clarity.

🔗 Google Sheets Sync

Connect your app to Google Sheets to back up all transactions automatically. Your data is always stored locally — Sheets sync is an optional cloud backup.
